What data were we given?

We were given seven different datasets each containing at least the following two columns:

  • post_date: A date between March 1st, 2020 and September 20th, 2021 when the job was posted.
  • count_id_indexed: The unique job listings for the dataset for a seven day period divided by the unique number of job listings for the dataset from February 24, 2020 to March 1, 2020.
